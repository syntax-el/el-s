HOBBIES AND INTERESTS

HOBBIES:

DRAWING

I enjoy creating drawings inspired by whatever ideas or images come to mind or spring from my imagination.

PHOTOGRAPHY

I love taking photos of things I can keep as memories, such as those related to astronomy, things I find cute and joyful-like cats and dogs-and my loved ones.

WRITING

I enjoy writing, especially random things-it feels like a habit I've grown used to.

GAMING

I love playing both online and offline games, like Mobile Legends, Roblox, Smurf's Village, Block Blast, Criminal Case, and Stardew Valley. I play mostly when I want entertainment or have free time.

READING

I love reading, whether it's books or e-books, but the feeling of reading a physical book is unmatched. I read for enjoyment and learning. I don’t know why, but whenever I read, it feels like I’m escaping into another world, far from reality. It’s such a light feeling. I like books about self-improvement or self-growth. I also enjoy books about crime thrillers, psychological thrillers, sci-fi, romance, drama, mystery, and fantasy.

WATCHING

I love watching, especially anime, movies, series, and cartoons. Studio Ghibli movies are my favorite. I enjoy ones where I can learn valuable lessons or simply have fun.

MUSIC

I enjoy listening to music; it helps me in every situation and in various ways depending on the mood-whether it calms me, provides emotional support, uplifts my mood, helps me relax, or releases stress.

INTERESTS:

ASTRONOMY

I love anything related to stars, the moon, the night sky, sunsets, and galaxies. I just find them comforting, almost unbelievable in their beauty, and incredibly amazing.

LANGUAGE LEARNING

I’m interested in learning new languages since it’s so exciting. It allows me to communicate with people from different countries. I also love the idea of making friends who live in other parts of the world or faraway places. It improves my communication skills and helps me connect with their cultures.

PUZZLE SOLVING

I’m really just interested in solving puzzles as it helps me think logically, use my critical thinking, and enhances my problem-solving skills. It also feels great when you solve them.

CODING/PROGRAMMING

I’m interested in this due to its relevance to the course I’m taking, even though I’m not that skilled yet, but I know there’s room for improvement. I also want to learn more in-depth knowledge about it. I just love how coding and programming work.
